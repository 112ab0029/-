Brand Story - Silva Pure

Sometimes, we are so busy that we forget that life can be light, quiet and natural.

Silva Pure was inspired by a quiet trip into the forest.

The air there is fresh, the sun shines through the leaves and falls on the palms, the smell of trees is mixed with the fragrance of grass and flowers, everything is so stable and so gentle.

We want to bring this feeling into our daily life.

So, we chose pure plant ingredients and non-irritating formulas, which can gently cleanse and quietly moisturize your fatigue throughout the day.

Just like the feeling of the forest, it is not ostentatious, but reassuring.

Silva means forest.

Silva Pure is a small ritual we want to give to everyone——

A gentle and clean way, so that you can feel the embrace of nature and quiet happiness in the moment of washing your hands.

Brand Concept

In the tranquility of the forest, we found the inspiration for cleansing and care.

Silva Pure promises to bring you not only a bottle of hand sanitizer, but also a daily experience that resonates with your skin and nature.

  • Strictly selected natural plant extracts
  • No artificial fragrances and irritating ingredients
  • Wash and moisturize your hands
  • Every time you wash your hands, it feels like a dance with nature

Product Features

  • Natural antibacterial formula: plant-derived ingredients, effective antibacterial and gentle without irritation
  • Skin-friendly repair: Contains organic aloe vera and calendula essence, moisturizing and soothing without dryness
  • Multiple styles of fragrance: blended with natural essential oils to meet different temperaments and preferences
  • Environmentally friendly bottle design: recyclable materials, beauty and earth sustainability coexist
  • Purity commitment: no animal testing, no silicone, no artificial colors
